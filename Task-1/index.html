About the NGO

About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation works for underprivileged communities through social welfare, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and skill development.

The foundation is registered under Section 8, licensed by the Central Government, and is listed as 80G and 12A certified, CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog registered, and ISO 9001:2015 certified on its official website.

Ongoing initiatives

Projects That Turn Care Into Action

InAmigos brings young volunteers, professionals, and communities together through six active projects focused on long-term social impact.

01

Project SEVA

Distributes food, clothes, and basic support to people in need, promoting dignity and relief.

02

Project BACHPANSHALA

Supports underprivileged children with digital literacy, life skills, and school education help.

03

Project JEEV

Focuses on animal welfare by feeding, caring for, and protecting stray animals.

04

Project UDAAN

Empowers women through skill development, financial independence, and menstrual hygiene awareness.

05

Project PRAKRITI

Promotes environmental conservation through plantation, sustainability, and eco-friendly action.

06

Project VIKAS

Improves employability through internships and training in research, marketing, content, finance, and social work.

Social purpose

Why These Projects Matter

These initiatives address everyday challenges faced by vulnerable communities: hunger, lack of learning access, unemployment, gender inequality, environmental damage, and animal neglect.

The purpose is not only short-term help. InAmigos aims to build self-reliance, awareness, compassion, and community participation so people can grow with dignity.
